

# Vehicle Type Analysis Insights

## Key Findings

* All vehicle categories generated comparable booking values, ranging from approximately **₹3.21M to ₹3.33M**.
* **Bike** and **Prime Sedan** contributed the highest booking values at approximately **₹3.33M** each.
* Average ride distance remained consistent across vehicle types, ranging between **25.15 km and 25.65 km**.
* Total distance travelled varied between **80K and 84K km**, indicating balanced utilization across vehicle categories.

## Business Recommendations

* Increase vehicle availability for high-performing categories such as Bike and Prime Sedan during peak demand periods.
* Evaluate pricing strategies for lower-performing vehicle categories to improve utilization.
* Continue monitoring ride distance patterns to optimize fleet allocation.
