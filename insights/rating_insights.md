

# Ratings Analysis Insights

## Key Findings

* Customer ratings remained highly consistent across all vehicle categories, averaging approximately **4.00 out of 5**.
* Driver ratings also remained stable, ranging between **4.00 and 4.01**.
* Prime SUV, Prime Plus, and Auto achieved some of the highest average ratings.
* No significant rating differences were observed between vehicle categories, indicating consistent service quality.

## Business Recommendations

* Maintain current service standards to preserve customer satisfaction levels.
* Identify and reward top-rated drivers to encourage service excellence.
* Collect qualitative feedback to uncover opportunities for improving the customer experience beyond ratings.
